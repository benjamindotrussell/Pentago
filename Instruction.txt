Instruction to run the Game

Start:
Click run.

The console will prompt you to Enter 1 for min-max or 2 for alpha-beta
type you choice into the console.

The console will prompt you to enter your name.
Enter your name.

The console will prompt you to choose white or black as your game piece.
type you choice into the console.
The game will ask you to reenter a selection of you enter anything but white or black.

The game will start at this point, selecting randomly who will go first.
when it prompts you to enter you move, please enter it in the form specified in the assignment
instructions '1/1 1r' there is some validation of your selection but it's not perfect.

The game will continue in the manner taking turns with you until it sees 5 pegs in a row,
at witch time it will declare a winner.
